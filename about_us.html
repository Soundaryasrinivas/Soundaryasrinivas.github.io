About Us

Overview

Viga Applications is led by visionary entrepreneurs with more than 30 years of experience in business management of engineering solutions for a wide spectrum of industries in areas such as Energy (Solar/Water/Audits and consultancy), Mining, Supply of Products and Solutions to a wide range of clientele. The company’s board of directors have successfully won the trust and confidence of many local & international clients for their professional management skills and personal values. Their vast experience in managing profitable and growing business operations, developing international joint ventures/consortium, successfully delivering multimillion dollar projects coupled with strong relationship network, have helped Viga Applications to accelerate its sustainable vertical and horizontal growth.

Mission

"To Provide Robust, Affordable, Scalable and Sustainable Engineering Solutions to Customers without compromising on our Core Values (Integrity, Honesty, Trust, Quality, Health, Safety and Environment)"

QHSE Policy


The safety and wellbeing of employees and contractors is non-negotiable. No employee or contractor will be expected, directed or encouraged to submit themselves or others to any uncontrolled risk in order to satisfy a work-related requirement.

Viga is committed to designing and constructing safe, reliable and environmentally friendly facilities. All Viga employees are expected to embrace these values and nurture a 100% safe culture. Viga will not waver in its commitment to its health, safety and environmental values.
